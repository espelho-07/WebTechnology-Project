Why travel with Way Toghether?

Ride options

There’s more than one way to move with Uber, no matter where you are or where you’re headed next.

15,000+ cities

The app is available in thousands of cities worldwide, so you can request a ride even when you’re far from home.

700+ airports

You can request a ride to and from most major airports. Schedule a ride to the airport for one less thing to worry about.

Benifits

PLATFORM DEDICATED TO YOUR ORGANIZATION

Development of specific modules for your needs. Creation of specific integrations with your IT tools and possibility of hosting on your own servers.

SECURE CARPOOLING TOOL

Your data is secure and hosted on ethical, RGPD-compliant servers in Switzerland. No data is used for commercial purposes.

ADVICE & SUPPORT

Analysis of your needs and design of your customized carpooling platform together. Consulting, mobility planning, and support for managing change.
