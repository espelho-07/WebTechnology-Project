The Carpooling Doodle

GoTogether makes carpooling easy to organize.

GoTogether is available in several solutions:
GoTogether Basic for small social events, GoTogether Plus for large professional events, and GoTogether Pro for companies and organizations with specific needs.

OUR SOLUTIONS

GoTogether BASIC
Ideal for travelling to tournaments, birthday parties or weddings.

✅ Event map
✅ Waiting list
✅ Trip search
☑️ Admin roles
☑️ Customized alerts
☑️ Data security
☑️ Secure management without password creation

GoTogether PLUS
Ideal for festivals, conferences and corporate events.

✅ Event map
✅ Waiting list
✅ Trip search
✅ Admin roles
✅ Customized alerts
✅ Data security
✅ Secure management without password creation

GoTogether PRO
Ideal for company carpooling and their specific needs.

✨ Statistics
✨ CO₂ Impact
✨ Carpool for commuting
✨ Multi-site carpooling
✨ Ticketing integration
✨ Optimized parking
✨ Customized branding
