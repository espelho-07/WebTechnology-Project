Questions About Publishing?

Our suggested price is based on the distance of your trip and average fuel and maintenance costs. It's a recommendation to keep your ride competitive and fair, but you always have the final say on the price you set.

After the trip is completed, the passenger's payment is securely transferred to your registered bank account or UPI. This typically takes 2-3 business days. You can track all your earnings in the 'My Profile' section.

Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both drivers and passengers. If a passenger cancels with sufficient notice, their seat becomes available for another booking. If they cancel late, you may still receive a portion of the payment. Please see our full cancellation policy in the Help Center.
