Driver Safety

We empower you with the tools and information you need to drive with confidence and peace of mind.

Features to Keep You Safe

You Choose Your Passengers

Review passenger profiles, ratings, and verifications before accepting a booking. You are in full control of who rides in your car.

Insurance Protection

While your personal car insurance is primary, we provide an additional layer of liability insurance for every SahaYana trip, at no extra cost to you.

24/7 Support

Our support team is always available. If you ever feel unsafe or need assistance during a trip, you can contact us directly through the app.

Best Practices for a Safe Drive

Use the in-app messaging to confirm pickup details and align on expectations. Clear communication prevents misunderstandings.

Regularly check your tires, brakes, and fluid levels. A well-maintained car is a safer car for you and your passengers.

If a situation or a booking request feels uncomfortable, you have the right to decline it. Your comfort and safety are paramount.
