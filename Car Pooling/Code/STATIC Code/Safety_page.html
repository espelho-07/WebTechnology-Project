Our Community Guidelines

Safety is a shared responsibility. We expect everyone in the SahaYana community to be respectful, reliable, and responsible.

Be Respectful

Treat your fellow members and their vehicles with respect. We have zero tolerance for discrimination or harassment of any kind.

Be on Time

Respect everyone's schedule by being punctual for pickups and drop-offs. Communicate any delays through the app.

Drive Safely

Drivers must adhere to all traffic laws, ensure their vehicle is well-maintained, and never drive under the influence.
