Your Journey, Simplified

Joining our carpooling community is as easy as 1-2-3. Find out how to get started below.

For Passengers

Find a comfortable and affordable ride in just a few taps.

1. Search for a Ride

Enter your departure and destination points, and your travel date to see all available rides.

2. Choose & Book

Select the ride that best suits you. Check the driver's profile, ratings, and vehicle details, then book your seat.

3. Travel Together

Meet your driver at the agreed pickup point. Enjoy the journey, share stories, and make new connections.

4. Rate Your Ride

After your trip, leave a rating for your driver to help build a more trustworthy community for everyone.

For Drivers

Turn your empty seats into earnings by sharing your ride.

1. Publish Your Ride

Easily offer a ride in seconds. Set your route, schedule, and the price per seat. Youâ€™re in full control.

2. Approve Bookings

Review passenger profiles and ratings, then accept booking requests. You decide who travels with you.

3. Drive & Share

Enjoy the company of your passengers, make your drive more interesting, and reduce your carbon footprint.

4. Get Paid Securely

After the ride, the payment is automatically transferred to your account. No cash handling needed.

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Yes, creating an account on SahaYana is completely free for both passengers and drivers. You only pay for the rides you take, and as a driver, you earn money by sharing your trips.

All payments are handled securely and cashlessly through the SahaYana app. Passengers pay when their booking is confirmed, and we hold the payment until the ride is completed, after which it's transferred to the driver.

Both passengers and drivers can cancel a ride through the app. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both parties. Please refer to our Help Center for detailed information on cancellation timelines and potential fees.
