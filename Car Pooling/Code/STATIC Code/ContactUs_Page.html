Frequently Asked Questions

We strive to answer all email inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent issues that require immediate attention, we recommend calling our support line.

A cancellation fee may be charged if a trip is canceled after a driver has already accepted and is on their way to your location. This compensates our drivers for their time and fuel. You can review the full details in our cancellation policy.

Please ensure the promo code was entered correctly and has not expired. If it still doesn't work, contact us with the promo code and your ride details, and we'll be happy to investigate the issue for you.

A receipt for every completed trip is automatically sent to your registered email address. You can also view and download receipts from the 'My Bookings' or ride history section of the app.

The quickest way to retrieve a lost item is to contact the driver directly through the app's ride history. If you're unable to connect after 24 hours, please contact our support team with your ride details, and we will assist you.
