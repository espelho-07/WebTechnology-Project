Moving Together, Growing Together

SahaYana is redefining shared mobility with trust, simplicity, and heart.

Our Mission

To build a community-driven platform that brings people together through shared rides, mutual trust, and sustainable travel. We envision a world where daily commutes become safer, more social, and more meaningful.

At SahaYana, our mission is to empower daily commuters with safe, reliable, and affordable rides through the spirit of sharing. We envision a future where every seat matters, every journey counts, and every user belongs.

Our Story

Born from the vision of creating a safe and affordable way to travel, SahaYana was founded to connect individuals going in the same direction. Our roots are in real-world challenges—college students, office workers, and travelers who simply wanted a better way to move.

SahaYana was born from a simple observation during college days—daily commuting wasn’t just tiring, but also expensive and lonely. Why drive solo when there are others heading the same way? What if strangers became fellow riders, and rides became connections?
