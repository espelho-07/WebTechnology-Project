Our Core Principles

Be Respectful

Treat your fellow members with respect and courtesy. We have zero tolerance for discrimination, harassment, or aggressive behavior.

Be Reliable

Be on time, whether you're a driver or a passenger. If you need to cancel, do so as early as possible. Reliability is key to a trustworthy community.

Prioritize Safety

Drivers must have a valid license and insurance, and their vehicle must be in good condition. All members must wear a seatbelt. Do not ask your driver to break traffic laws.

Uphold Cost-Sharing

SahaYana is for sharing costs, not for profit. Drivers should not use the platform as a commercial taxi service. Keep all transactions within the app for security.

See Something, Say Something.

If you encounter behavior that violates these guidelines, please don't hesitate to report it. Your action helps protect the entire community.
