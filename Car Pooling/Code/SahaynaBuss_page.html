SahaYana for Business

A smarter, more affordable, and sustainable travel solution for your employees.

Why Choose SahaYana for Your Company?

Reduce Costs

Drastically cut your company's travel and reimbursement expenses by leveraging the power of carpooling for employee commutes and business trips.

Boost Sustainability

Meet your corporate social responsibility goals by reducing your company's carbon footprint. Fewer cars on the road means a greener planet.

Enhance Employee Morale

Carpooling fosters connections and collaboration among colleagues, turning a stressful commute into a positive social experience.

How It Works

1. Company Onboarding

We'll set up a dedicated carpooling portal for your company and help you configure your travel policies.

2. Employee Registration

Employees sign up using their corporate email, ensuring a secure and verified community of colleagues.

3. Create & Join Rides

Employees can easily offer rides in their own vehicles or find colleagues traveling along the same route.
